Vinland Saga

Synopsis:

Vinland Saga is a historical action manga set during the Viking Age, following the journey of Thorfinn, a young warrior driven by a desire for revenge against Askeladd, the man who killed his father. The story begins with Thorfinn's relentless pursuit of vengeance, but as the plot unfolds, it delves deeper into themes of war, honor, and the consequences of violence.

Alongside Thorfinn, the manga explores the lives of historical figures and the harsh realities of Viking life, including political intrigue and the rise of powerful rulers.
