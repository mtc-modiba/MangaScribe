Nisekoi

Synopsis:

Nisekoi is a romantic comedy manga that follows Raku Ichijou, the heir to a powerful yakuza family, and Chitoge Kirisaki, the daughter of a rival gang leader. Due to a misunderstanding, Raku and Chitoge are forced into a fake relationship to prevent a feud between their families.

However, both of them are determined to keep their true feelings hidden, especially since Raku is secretly in love with his childhood friend, whom he made a promise to years ago but has forgotten the identity of.

As the story unfolds, Raku becomes entangled in a love triangle (and eventually a love pentagon) as he navigates his feelings for several girls, leading to comedic and heartwarming moments.
