Berserk

Synopsis:

Berserk is a dark fantasy manga that follows the story of Guts, a lone mercenary with a tragic past, and his journey through a brutal, war-torn world. The series explores his quest for vengeance against Griffith, a former friend turned ruthless leader, who betrays him and sacrifices their group for power. Themes of fate, ambition, and the human struggle between light and darkness are central to the story. As Guts battles both monstrous creatures and his inner demons, Berserk delves into complex characters, morality, and the consequences of seeking power at any cost. The series is known for its intense action, deep character development, and dark, often unsettling atmosphere.
