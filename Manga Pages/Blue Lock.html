Blue Lock

Synopsis:

Blue Lock is a sports manga centered around soccer, focusing on the intense competition to find Japan's next top striker.

After Japan's national soccer team fails to perform in the World Cup, the Japan Football Association launches a radical training program called Blue Lock. The program gathers 300 talented young strikers and pits them against each other in a brutal competition, with the goal of creating the most exceptional forward.

The story follows Yoichi Isagi, a high school player who joins Blue Lock, as he navigates the fierce rivalry, overcomes self-doubt, and strives to become the best striker.

The series explores themes of ego, ambition, and the drive to surpass one's limits.
