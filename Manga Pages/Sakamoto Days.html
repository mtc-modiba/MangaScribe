Sakamoto Days

Synopsis:

Sakamoto Days is an action-comedy manga that follows Taro Sakamoto, a former legendary hitman who has retired from the world of assassination to live a peaceful life as a convenience store owner. Despite his appearance as an average, overweight middle-aged man, Sakamoto still possesses exceptional combat skills.

His quiet life is disrupted when a former associate targets him, and he is dragged back into the world of dangerous assassins.

The series blends humor, action, and heartwarming moments as Sakamoto navigates his new life while protecting his family and employees, all while maintaining his desire for a peaceful existence.
