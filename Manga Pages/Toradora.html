Toradora!

Synopsis:

Toradora! is a romantic comedy and slice-of-life manga that centers around Ryuuji Takasu, a high school student with an intimidating face, and Taiga Aisaka, a small but fierce girl known as the "Palmtop Tiger."

Despite their differences, they form an unlikely friendship after realizing they each have a crush on the other's best friend. Ryuuji and Taiga agree to help each other with their romantic pursuits, but their relationship gradually deepens as they spend more time together.
