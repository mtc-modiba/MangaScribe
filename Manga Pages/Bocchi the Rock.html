Bocchi the Rock

Synopsis:

Bocchi the Rock! is a slice-of-life manga about Hitori "Bocchi" Gotou, a socially anxious high school girl who dreams of becoming a famous guitarist.

Despite her passion for music, Bocchi struggles with extreme shyness and often isolates herself.

Her life takes a turn when she meets the members of a high school rock band, Kessoku Band, and begins to connect with them. As she joins the band, Bocchi must confront her fears of social interaction and find a way to balance her passion for music with building meaningful friendships.
