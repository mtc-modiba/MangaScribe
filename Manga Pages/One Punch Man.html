One Punch Man

Synopsis:

One Punch Man is a superhero action manga that follows Saitama, an ordinary man who becomes so powerful that he can defeat any opponent with a single punch. Despite his overwhelming strength, Saitama is bored and frustrated by the lack of challenging opponents, leading him to join the Hero Association in hopes of finding a worthy adversary. As he faces various monsters and villains, Saitama’s struggle is not with his enemies, but with his own sense of purpose and the search for excitement in his otherwise mundane life.
