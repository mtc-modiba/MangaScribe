Death Note

Synopsis:

Death Note is a psychological thriller manga that follows Light Yagami, a highly intelligent high school student who discovers a mysterious notebook that grants the power to kill anyone whose name is written in it, as long as the writer knows the person’s face. Light, believing he can use the Death Note to rid the world of criminals and create a utopia under his rule, adopts the alias "Kira." This sparks a global manhunt led by a brilliant and eccentric detective known only as L. As Light and L engage in a high-stakes battle of wits, the series explores themes of justice, morality, and the consequences of absolute power.
