Dead Tube

Synopsis:

Dead Tube is a psychological horror and thriller manga that follows the story of Tetsuo, a high school student who becomes involved in a dangerous and twisted online video platform called "Dead Tube."

The platform encourages users to upload extreme, often violent content, and the more shocking the videos, the higher the rewards.

Tetsuo’s life takes a dark turn when he becomes entangled with his classmate, the beautiful and manipulative Mai, who uses the platform to expose and exploit people’s darkest desires.

As Tetsuo gets drawn deeper into the world of Dead Tube, he must navigate a deadly game of manipulation, secrets, and moral decay, all while trying to survive the horrifying consequences of his actions. The manga explores themes of obsession, voyeurism, and the destructive power of social media.
