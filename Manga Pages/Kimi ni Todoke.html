Kimi ni Todoke

Synopsis:

Kimi ni Todoke is a romantic shoujo manga that follows the story of Sawako Kuronuma, a shy and misunderstood high school girl often compared to the horror movie character Sadako due to her long black hair and quiet demeanor. Despite her kind nature, her classmates keep their distance because of her intimidating appearance. Her life begins to change when the popular and charismatic boy, Shouta Kazehaya, starts talking to her, gradually helping her open up and make new friends. As Sawako navigates the ups and downs of high school, she also begins to develop feelings for Kazehaya, leading to a sweet, heartfelt romance.
