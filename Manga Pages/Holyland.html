Holyland

Synopsis:

Holyland is a martial arts and psychological manga that follows the story of a troubled teenager named Yu Kamishiro. Struggling with bullying and isolation, Yu seeks refuge in the streets of Tokyo, where he begins training in street fighting to defend himself and find a sense of purpose. As he grows stronger, he becomes involved in the underground world of street fighting, where he faces off against various opponents with different fighting styles. The series delves into themes of personal growth, the search for identity, and the psychological effects of violence, while exploring the darker side of human nature.
