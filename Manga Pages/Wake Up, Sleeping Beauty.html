Wake Up, Sleeping Beauty

Synopsis:

Tetsu, a 16-year-old first year high school student just got a part time job as a housekeeper for a mansion surrounded by roses. A girl called Shizu lives there, who appears innocent and pure but lives haphazardly.

However, there seems to be a mystery surrounding her: who is the other girl that looks at Satoshi with a cold blank stare?
