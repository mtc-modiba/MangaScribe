Jujutsu Kaisen

Synopsis:

Jujutsu Kaisen is a supernatural action manga that follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student with extraordinary physical abilities.

After encountering a cursed object, Yuji unwittingly swallows a powerful cursed relic, the Finger of Ryomen Sukuna, a deadly curse that brings chaos and destruction.

To prevent Sukuna from wreaking havoc, Yuji joins the Tokyo Metropolitan Magic Technical College, where he trains as a jujutsu sorcerer under the guidance of Satoru Gojo. Alongside his friends and fellow sorcerers, Yuji battles dangerous curses and learns to harness his newfound powers.
