Grand Blue

Synopsis:

Grand Blue is a comedy and slice-of-life manga that follows Iori Kitahara, a college student who moves to a seaside town to start his new life. He soon becomes involved with the eccentric and wild members of the university's diving club, led by his uncle, who runs a small shop called Grand Blue.

Despite expecting a calm and studious life, Iori is swept into a world of outrageous drinking, wild parties, and absurd antics. The series combines hilarious and often outrageous humor with themes of friendship, self-discovery, and the chaos of young adulthood.
