Oyasumi Punpun

Synopsis:

Oyasumi Punpun is a coming-of-age drama manga that follows the life of Punpun Punyama, a young boy who grows up in a turbulent and often disturbing environment. The story is told through Punpun's experiences, focusing on his relationships with his family, friends, and the struggles he faces as he navigates the complexities of adolescence and adulthood.

The manga explores themes of mental health, trauma, loneliness, love, and the harsh realities of life, all while maintaining a deeply emotional and often surreal tone.

Oyasumi Punpun is known for its raw and unflinching portrayal of the human condition, with a unique art style that reflects the dark and poignant nature of the narrative.
